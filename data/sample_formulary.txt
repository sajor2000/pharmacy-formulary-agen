Sample Insurance Formulary

PREFERRED MEDICATIONS:

Short-Acting Rescue Inhalers (SABA):
- Albuterol Sulfate HFA (Generic) - Tier 1, No PA required
- Ventolin HFA - Tier 2, PA required

Inhaled Corticosteroids (ICS):
- QVAR RediHaler (beclomethasone) - Tier 2, No PA
- Pulmicort Flexhaler (budesonide) - Tier 2, Step therapy required

ICS-LABA Combinations:
- Wixela Inhub (fluticasone/salmeterol) - Tier 1, Preferred
- Advair Diskus - Tier 2, PA required
- Symbicort - Tier 2, Step therapy required

LAMA Medications:
- Spiriva HandiHaler (tiotropium) - Tier 2, PA required
- Incruse Ellipta (umeclidinium) - Tier 2, No PA

Quantity Limits:
- All inhalers: 1 inhaler per 30 days unless otherwise specified
- Rescue inhalers may be approved for 2 per 30 days with documentation

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. Failed trial of preferred alternative
2. Documentation of medical necessity
3. Specialist consultation notes if required
